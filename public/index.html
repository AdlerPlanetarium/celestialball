September 12, 2015

6:30 pm

RECEPTION & SILENT AUCTION

DINNER & DANCING

The Adler Planetarium Women's Board is the museum's lead partner in education. Through its efforts, the Women's Board makes possible programs and exhibitions that engage people of all ages in science and inspire young people to pursue careers in STEM.

Auction Highlights

Along with these fabulous auction highlights, this year's silent auction features one-of-a-kind experiences, incredible travel opportunities, and unique sports, home, and entertainment packages, plus much more!

  • Island Vacation

    Visit beautiful Anguilla for three nights in a premium oceanview room at exclusive resort Malliouhana. Enjoy direct access to three pristine beaches from your luxury accommodations, perched on a panoramic bluff overlooking the Caribbean Sea.

  • Blackhawks Tickets

    View the action up close with four “on the glass” tickets to a Blackhawks game during the 2015-16 regular season! Includes four “admittance only” passes to the Ketel One Club for dinner and a parking pass. Tickets are in section 121, row 1.

  • Fly to Asia, Africa, or Pacific

    A fabulous trip is in store for you as you and a guest fly round-trip, business class on United Airlines. You’ll have a difficult decision choosing between incredible locations in Africa, Asia, Australia, New Zealand, India and the Middle East. Prepare to embark on an excellent adventure!

  • Stratosphere Flight Experience

    Be a member of a Far Horizons Launch Crew in a once-in-a-lifetime stratospheric flight experience. Help launch an experiment flown to 100,000 feet on a high-altitude balloon. Day-long adventure for up to six people includes preparations and launch, an exciting chase following the balloon flight, and retrieval.

  • Personal Napa Valley Chauffeur

    Experience Napa the way few tourists do. Your personal driver will work with you to design a custom two-day tour of Napa Valley while helping you discover some of the lesser known wineries that are hidden gems and the best kept secrets of locals! Add in a two-night stay at St. Helena Winery for a perfect weekend away!

  • Blackhawks Suite

    Watch the action live from a lower-level suite at the United Center! You and 17 of your friends will get the VIP experience on Monday, October 26, 2015 to see the Hawks take on the Anaheim Ducks at the United Center. Suite includes 3 parking passes, catering, beverages, and dessert cart.

  • Adopt Adorable Astro!

    Meet Astro! He’s a beautiful yellow Labrador puppy looking for the perfect family to call his very own.

  • Magnificent Mallorca

    Revel in the stunning natural beauty found on the island of Mallorca, Spain, one of the great natural destinations of the Mediterranean. Includes two business class, round-trip tickets on United Airlines and a week-long stay at an incredible villa located near the five-star Gran Hotel Son Net with access to all of the hotel's facilities.

  • Chicago Bulls Tickets

    Watch the Bulls up close with four courtside chair seats to a 2015-16 regular season game! This package includes four "admission only" passes to the Ketel One Club and a parking pass.
