RECEPTION & SILENT AUCTION

The Adler Planetarium’s Women’s Board is the museum’s lead partner in education. Through its efforts, the Women’s Board makes possible programs and exhibitions that engage people of all ages in science and inspire young people to pursue careers in STEM.

Auction

  • Apollo 13 American Flag

    A 4”x6” American flag that flew onboard the Apollo 13 Command Module Odyssey, April 11-17, 1970, from the personal collection of Captain James A. Lovell, Jr.

  • Gorgeous GRAFF

    You, or that very special someone, will look stunning after you use your $10,000 gift certificate for one of GRAFF’s “most fabulous jewels in the world”

  • Spectacular Spain

    Visit Mallorca for a week-long stay at an incredible private villa. Your accommodations will be near the five-star Gran Hotel Son Net, with access to hotel’s facilities.

  • Science Hackshops: Building Telescopes

    Join the Adler for an exclusive multi-week workshop. Families will spend five weeks with museum experts learning how to see and observe the Universe; documenting their observations and the process of discovery; and constructing a high-quality telescope to take home and enjoy for years to come.
