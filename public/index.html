2013 Celestial Ball

Hosted by the Women's Board of the Adler Planetarium

The Adler Planetarium’s annual black-tie gala will be held against the stunning backdrop of Chicago’s skyline and Lake Michigan at night. The Celestial Ball is attended by more than 600 business, civic, and philanthropic leaders and raises more than $1 million for the Adler’s STEM education programs. Guests will enjoy cocktails, fine cuisine, and dancing. Our highly anticipated event will be an elegant evening and a highlight of the autumn season.
