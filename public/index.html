The Adler Planetarium’s Women’s Board is the museum’s lead partner in education. Through its efforts, the Women’s Board makes possible programs and exhibitions that engage people of all ages in science and inspire young people to pursue careers in STEM.

Auction Highlights

Along with these fabulous auction highlights, this year’s silent auction features one–of–a–kind experiences, incredible travel opportunities, and unique sports, home, and entertainment packages, plus much more!

  • Apollo 13 American Flag

    A 4”x6” American flag that flew onboard the Apollo 13 Command Module Odyssey, April 11-17, 1970, from the personal collection of Captain James A. Lovell, Jr.

  • Gorgeous GRAFF

    You, or that very special someone, will look stunning after you use your $10,000 gift certificate for one of GRAFF’s “most fabulous jewels in the world”

  • Magnificent Mallorca

    Visit Mallorca for a week-long stay at an incredible private villa. Your accommodations will be near the five-star Gran Hotel Son Net, with access to the hotel’s facilities. Two round-trip, business class tickets on United Airlines included.

  • Science Hackshops: Building Telescopes

    Join the Adler for an exclusive multi-week workshop. Families will spend five weeks with museum experts learning how to see and observe the Universe; documenting their observations and the process of discovery; and constructing a high-quality telescope to take home and enjoy for years to come.

  • Puppy Love

    Meet Chewy! This handsome Chocolate Havanese puppy is AKC-registered with champion bloodlines. Small, intelligent, and gentle, this cutie is an ideal companion dog for the city or the suburbs. Naturally hypoallergenic, he is registered with current vaccinations, veterinarian examinations, a health certificate and guarantee, and pedigree.

  • SpaceX Rocket Factory Tour

    You and up to 8 guests will enjoy a private tour of the SpaceX rocket ad spacecraft factory headquarters in Hawthorne, California just minutes from Los Angeles International Airport. There you will experience an exclusive tour the world’s largest facility performing the complete design, fabrication, and assembly of rockets, engines, and spacecraft.

  • The Suite Life

    Up to 18 guests will enjoy a fully catered United Center suite for the Chicago Blackhawks home game versus Calgary Flames on Wednesday, October 15. Includes food, beverages, and two parking passes.

  • Hawaiian Paradise

    The islands are calling you! Enjoy a seven-day, six-night stay at a beautiful private residence. This luxury home is located in the pristine North Shore area of the island overlooking Ho’okipa Beach, and features a gourmet kitchen, infinity pool and hot tub overlooking the ocean, huge outdoor lanai with outdoor dining and lounge area with a beautiful courtyard. Two round-trip, business class tickets on United Airlines included.

  • Fly to Asia, Africa, or Pacific

    A fabulous trip is in store for you as you and a guest fly round-trip, business class on United Airlines. You’ll have a difficult decision choosing between incredible locations in Africa, Asia, Australia, New Zealand, India and the Middle East. Prepare to embark on an excellent adventure!

  • Tucson Weekend Stay

    You and nine guests will enjoy four days and three “starry nights” at IJ Bar ranch, a private working ranch one hour outside of Tucson, Arizona. Enjoy horseback riding, tennis, skeet shooting, swimming, and relaxing, all within the property confines. Ranch includes four guest suites, and four bunk rooms.

  • Cubs VIP Experience

    You will be treated to an exclusive behind-the-scenes tour that is not available to the public and will be able to catch a game from amazing seats. Package includes four Club Box infield tickets to a Chicago Cubs game, a private tour of Wrigley Field for four guests, Cubs batting practice and photos on-deck prior to the game during the 2014 or 2015 season.

  • American Girl

    For the special little girl in your life, bring home the no longer available “2013 Girl of the Year” American Girl doll, Saige. This high-flying package includes a beautiful, colorful inflatable nylon hot air balloon, faux binoculars, a mini map of Albuquerque, a pilot’s log book, faux camera, and a messenger bag that’s roomy enough to store everything inside.

  • Believe in the Bears!

    How the Bears will do this season is anyone’s guess, but you can be there to show your support from these amazing seats! Includes, six 40-yard line tickets and one premium parking pass for the home game against the New Orleans Saints on Monday, December 15, 2014.

  • Stratosphere Flight Experience

    Be a member of a Far Horizons Launch Crew in a once-in-a-lifetime stratospheric flight experience. Help to launch an experiment flown to 100,000 feet on a high-altitude balloon. Day-long adventure for up to six family members, friends, or colleagues includes van ride to Koerner Aviation in Kankakee for preparations and launch, an exciting chase following the balloon flight, and retrieval. Lunch included. Participants can send personal artifacts to near space and will receive a DVD from the flight.

  • Personal Napa Valley Chauffeur

    Experience Napa the way few tourists do. Your personal driver will work with you to design a custom two-day tour of Napa while helping you discover some of the lesser known wineries that are worth the trip. Includes a $500 gift certificate for a stay at the Vintage Inn, a AAA Four Diamondaward-winning Napa Valley Hotel, a picnic lunch at one of the vineyards, and a mixed case of Napa Valley red wines.

  • Private Tour of Large Millimeter Telescope, Puebla, Mexico

    Accompany scientists on a unique tour for eight of the Large Millimeter Telescope and visit the High Altitdue Water Chernkov Observatory.The 50m-diameter LMT is the world´s largest single-dish steerable millimeter-wavelength telescope. Situated at an altitude of 15,000 feet, the LMT engages in exploration of the physical processes that lead to the formation and evolution of planetary systems, stars, blackholes and galaxies. The High Altitude Water Cherenkov Observatory is designed to observe TeV gamma rays and cosmic rays, which are markers of the most extreme environments in the known universe: supernova explosions, active galactic nuclei, and gamma-ray bursts.
