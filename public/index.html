Please join the Women’s Board of the Adler Planetarium for a Over the Moon, the 2015 Celestial Ball

September 12, 2015

6:30 pm

RECEPTION & SILENT AUCTION

DINNER & DANCING

The Adler Planetarium’s Women’s Board is the museum’s lead partner in education. Through its efforts, the Women’s Board makes possible programs and exhibitions that engage people of all ages in science and inspire young people to pursue careers in STEM.

Auction Highlights

Along with these fabulous auction highlights, this year’s silent auction features one–of–a–kind experiences, incredible travel opportunities, and unique sports, home, and entertainment packages, plus much more!

  • Island Vacation

    Visit beautiful Anguilla for three nights in a premium oceanview room at exclusive resort Malliouhana. Enjoy direct access to three pristine beaches from your luxury accommodations, perched on a panoramic bluff overlooking the Carribean Sea.

  • Blackhawks Tickets

    View the action up close with four “on the glass” tickets to a Blackhawks game during the 2015-16 regular season! Includes four “admittance only” passes to the Ketel One Club for dinner and a parking pass. Tickets are in section 121, row 1.

  • Fly to Asia, Africa, or Pacific

    A fabulous trip is in store for you as you and a guest fly round-trip, business class on United Airlines. You’ll have a difficult decision choosing between incredible locations in Africa, Asia, Australia, New Zealand, India and the Middle East. Prepare to embark on an excellent adventure!

  • Stratosphere Flight Experience

    Be a member of a Far Horizons Launch Crew in a once-in-a-lifetime stratospheric flight experience. Help to launch an experiment flown to 100,000 feet on a high-altitude balloon. Day-long adventure for up to six family members, friends, or colleagues includes van ride to Koerner Aviation in Kankakee for preparations and launch, an exciting chase following the balloon flight, and retrieval. Lunch included. Participants can send personal artifacts to near space and will receive a DVD from the flight.

  • Personal Napa Valley Chauffeur

    Experience Napa the way few tourists do. Your personal driver will work with you to design a custom two-day tour of Napa while helping you discover some of the lesser known wineries that are worth the trip.
